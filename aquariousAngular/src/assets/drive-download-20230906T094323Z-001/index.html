Craft in focusDokra

Dokra craft is a form of brassware handicraft that originated in India and is practiced by artisans belonging to the Dhokra caste. It is an ancient craft that has been passed down through generations, and is believed to have originated in the Bastar district of Chhattisgarh.

The process of making dokra craft involves the lost-wax casting technique, which involves creating a clay model of the desired product and then covering it with a layer of beeswax mixed with resin and clay.
